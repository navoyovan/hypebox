# Design Direction

## Visual Style

* premium editorial minimalist
* cinematic layouts
* highly readable yet beautiful color palette
* large typography
* psycologically engaging contrast
* editorial spacing

## Inspirations

* Agence Foudre
* modern creative agencies
* fashion editorials
* experimental portfolios

## Layout Notes

* avoid overly centered layouts
* use image rhythm and pacing
* combine structured and offset sections
* prioritize visual hierarchy

## Components

### Buttons

* subtle hover transitions
* minimal borders
* clean typography

### Cards

* low visual noise
* soft separation
* avoid heavy shadows

### Motion

* slow and deliberate
* opacity and transform preferred
* stagger subtly
* avoid bouncy motion
