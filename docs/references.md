# References & Inspiration

Inspirations, layout pacing systems, and design references used to establish the visual aesthetics.

## Design Inspirations

1. **Agence Foudre** (Foudre.co)
   - Large typography contrast, stark black and white aesthetics, massive headers.
2. **Modern Creative Agencies**
   - Asymmetric offset grids where layout blocks overlap or break regular columns.
3. **Fashion & Art Editorials**
   - Generous breathing room, vertical accent lines, metadata descriptions paired with clean imagery.

## Layout Rhythms

- **Visual Rhythm**: Alternating visual weights as the user scrolls. A large wide image followed by two small side-by-side offset details, followed by a text-only spread.
- **Micro-accents**: Simple borders (`border-white/10` or `border-neutral-900`) that act as thin separator rules, resembling architectural blue-prints or printed magazines.
- **Asymmetric Grid Spans**:
  - Item 1: `col-span-12 md:col-span-7`
  - Item 2: `col-span-12 md:col-span-4 md:col-start-9 mt-12`
  - Item 3: `col-span-12 md:col-span-8 md:col-start-3`
