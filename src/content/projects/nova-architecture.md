## Framing Concrete and Space

The Nova Architecture Portal is a visual archive built for concrete minimalist structures. Our visual direction focused on **asymmetrical balancing**, heavy typography contrast, and bold editorial negative space.

### Design Principles in Code

1. **Editorial Layout Rhythms**: By implementing an offset grid, we created an asymmetrical scroll experience where layouts feel like physical magazine pages.
2. **Accessible Typography**: We utilized large, highly readable headers in Bricolage Grotesque, backed by high contrast ratios (exceeding WCAG AAA standards) for maximum legibility.
3. **Subtle Motion Choreography**: Scroll-based interactions are limited to soft opacities and subtle translation changes, respecting the user's focus.
